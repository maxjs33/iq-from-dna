About Us

Our Mission

At IQ from DNA, we're committed to making advances in genomic science accessible and meaningful to individuals. By translating complex genetic data into understandable insights about cognitive potential, we aim to empower people with knowledge that can inform educational and personal development decisions.

Our Approach

We believe in the responsible application of genetic science. Our approach is:

  • Research-driven: We base our algorithms on peer-reviewed research and continually update our methods as the science evolves.
  • Transparent: We clearly communicate what our estimates can and cannot tell you, including their limitations.
  • Privacy-focused: Your genetic data belongs to you. We implement state-of-the-art security measures and never share your data.
  • Educational: We aim to inform and educate, helping users understand the complex interplay between genetics and cognition.

Our Values

Scientific Integrity

We maintain rigorous scientific standards and are transparent about the capabilities and limitations of our methods.

Ethical Practice

We adhere to strict ethical guidelines in handling genetic data and presenting results to users.

Accessibility

We believe scientific advances should be understandable and accessible to all, not just specialists.

Individual Empowerment

We provide information that helps individuals make informed decisions about their personal development.
